DeepGreen

The project DeepGreen aims to transfer scientific publications, which can be made freely available after the end of their embargo periods, into Open Acess repositories. The focus over the next two years will be on publications with Alliance licenses.

For this, DeepGreen will create a data hub, which enables publishers to automatically deposit publications (incl. metadata) into corresponding Open Access repositories. At the moment this responsibility is in the hands of the authors and/or their institutional representatives, which can deposit their publications manually into repositories. However this action is rarely taken.

The DeepGreen Test Publications Router is based on the JISC Publications Router in England, which is a similar data hub like the one DeepGreen is trying to create.

Some information was taken from the official JISC Publications Router Website but has, in many cases, been adapted to the project.
