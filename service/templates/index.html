DeepGreen

The DeepGreen project aims to transfer scientific publications, which are subject to Alliance licences and thus entitled to be made freely available at the end of their respective embargo periods, into Open Access repositories.

In order to make this Open Access transformation happen DeepGreen has developed a data hub which enables publishers to automatically deposit publications (including metadata), which will then be routed to eligible Open Access repositories.

The DeepGreen data hub is based on the British Jisc Publications Router, a service institutions can use to obtain publications for their repositories from Open Access feeds and registered publishers.

Reliable and up-to-date information about the specific rights and conditions of each individual Alliance licence are provided by the Electronic Journals Library (EZB) This enables DeepGreen to automatically check whether an individual article is actually subject to an Alliance licence before taking any further processing steps.
