The DeepGreen project aims to transfer scientific publications, which are entitled to be made freely available at the end of their respective embargo periods, into Open Access repositories. These legitimate Open Access transformations are subject to Alliance licenses. The allowance of these transformations will carefully be assessed by DeepGreen.

For this service, you find here the entry point to a data hub, which enables publishers to automatically deposit publications (incl. metadata) into qualified Open Access repositories.

The DeepGreen prototype is based on the British JISC Publications Router, a service for interested institutions to obtain publications for their repositories from Open Access feeds and registered publishers.

Additionally, mircoservices kindly provided by the Electronic Journals Library (EZB) are helping DeepGreen in parts, to combine reliably the Alliance licenses information with the successful OA transformation.
